Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2024

What Information We Collect

When you use SydneyBuddy, we collect:

  • Your location preferences (to recommend appropriate phone plans)
  • Usage requirements (to match you with suitable data plans)
  • Length of stay (to suggest the most cost-effective options)
  • Device information (browser type and version)

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Provide personalized phone plan recommendations
  • Show you relevant store locations
  • Improve our recommendations based on user patterns
  • Understand how users interact with our website

Analytics and Tracking

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Features used and buttons clicked
  • General location data (city level only)
  • Device and browser information

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • We don't store any payment information
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • We only collect the minimum information needed to help you find a plan
  • We don't sell or share your personal information with third parties

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at:

  • Email: privacy@sydneybuddy.com
  • Website: Through our contact form
